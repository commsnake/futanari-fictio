2. He clutches his meager belongings, anxious about the $15,000 gang debt he's running from.
3. Leo boards the vessel, immediately struck by the scale of the reinforced F-Designation refits.
4. He is intercepted on deck by Morgan Thorne, the First Mate.
5. Morgan's imposing physical presence (6'1", severely toned) immediately triggers Leo's defensive sarcasm; he snarks that he thought he was meeting the Captain.

7. She leads him to the Captain's quarters (or a makeshift office area) to finalize the paperwork.
8. Morgan presents the "Articles of Employment" contract to Leo.
9. Leo skims the contract, noting the heavy demands (high-capacity galley management, "put it right" CNC clauses).
10. Desperate to escape his gang debt and blinded by the high sign-on bonus, he signs it, believing he can just jump ship later.
11. Morgan formally witnesses the signature.
12. Morgan explains the mandatory safety protocol due to the engine and deck noise: the Visual Safeword.
13. She demonstrates the double-tap to the dominant shoulder, making him practice it.
14. She coldly informs him of his absolute bottom-tier rank as "Super-Greenhorn."
15. Morgan leads him below deck into the claustrophobic crew quarters.
16. She assigns him his berth: the loudest, wettest bunk directly adjacent to the vibrating engine room.
17. Leo notices the reinforced steel framing and high-density memory foam of the bunks, though he doesn't understand why yet.
18. He drops his bag on the bunk, feeling the oppressive vibration of the ship's generators.
19. Before he can unpack or settle, Morgan issues his first direct order.
20. She instructs him to immediately report to the galley to begin prepping the massive caloric intake needed for the crew's departure.
21. Leo hesitates, an old instinct of resistance flaring up.
22. Morgan steps into his personal space, an unspoken threat of the newly signed contract hanging in the air.
23. Leo swallows his pride, intimidated by her proximity and the absolute authority she wields.
24. He nods, grabbing an apron or towel, and heads toward the galley.
25. The chapter ends with Leo feeling the engines rumble to life, realizing he is trapped on the high seas with a crew he doesn't understand.
