# KB-01: Narrative Architecture & Pacing

This module defines the structural constraints and intensity ladders for high-fidelity erotic narrative production.

## 1. The 8-Phase Erotic Cycle
All explicit scenes must graduate through these eight phases to ensure psychological immersion and narrative weight.

| Phase | Title | Objective |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **The Hook** | Ignition point; a look, a word, or a sudden realization of desire. |
| **2** | **The Lead-In** | Sensual build; focused on sensory precursors (smell, heat, friction). |
| **3** | **Dynamic Shift** | The transfer of power; one partner takes authority, the other surrenders. |
| **4** | **The Core Act** | Initial physical contact/penetration; focus on "Anatomic Anchor" words. |
| **5** | **The Escalation** | Rising intensity; faster pace, shorter sentences, higher sensory density. |
| **6** | **The Climax** | The moment of release; multi-wave internal/external description. |
| **7** | **The Melt** | The collapse; post-orgasmic shaking and metabolic recalibration. |
| **8** | **Aftercare** | Re-connection; soft touch, emotional shielding, and sanctuary building. |

## 2. H-KIS Intensity Levels (Heat-Kink-Intensity-Saturation)
The algorithmic switch for content calibration.

- **Level 1 (Exploratory & Sensual)**: Mutual discovery, gentle touch, emotional connection. No graphic acts.
- **Level 2 (Thematic Power Play)**: Consensual power dynamics, teasing, light discipline, playful dominance.
- **Level 3 (Hardcore Dynamics)**: Intense acts, high sensory detail, multi-partner orchestration.
- **Level 4 (Edgeplay & CNC)**: High-tension, consensual reality roleplay, psychological heavy-lifting.
- **Level 5 (Transgressive & Permanent)**: Absolute surrender, extreme taboos, deep psychological impact.

## 3. The Heat Ladder (Pacing)
Correlation between manuscript length and erotic density.

- **Heat 1-3**: Erotic elements are subtextual or "Closed Door."
- **Heat 4-6**: 1-2 key scenes per arc; focus on romantic buildup. **Futanari Specific:** The build-up must focus on hiding the anatomy, creating tension through forced proximity or near-miss "accidental reveals."
- **Heat 7-9**: Explicit, phase-aware scenes (The 8-Phase Cycle). **Futanari Specific:** The "Reveal" acts as the Catalyst (Phase 3: Dynamic Shift). The shock must quickly pivot to intense arousal and acceptance.
- **Heat 10**: High-saturation "Smut" density; erotic beats drive the plot directly. **Futanari Specific:** Heavy focus on anatomical mechanics, size kink, stamina, and volume. The erotic cycle may repeat rapidly without full aftercare between acts.
