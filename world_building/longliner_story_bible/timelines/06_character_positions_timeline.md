# 06. Character Positions Timeline

This document maps the standard locations and shifts of the crew throughout "The Deep Water Haul" arc. It aligns with `00_overarching_timeline_and_sea_conditions.md` to ensure realistic physical placement based on the journey's time and operational state.

## Default Daily Shifts (Offshore Operations)
When the vessel is actively fishing (lines out), the ship operates on a brutal 24-hour cycle.

*   **Captain:** Wheelhouse (Navigation/Sonar) or Captain's Quarters. Rarely on deck unless a crisis occurs.
*   **First Mate:** Roaming. Splits time between the Wheelhouse, managing the Deck Boss, and administrative duties in the Galley or her quarters.
*   **Engineer:** Engine Room. Constantly monitoring the propulsion, generators, and hydraulic systems.
*   **Deck Boss:** Main Deck (Aft/Setting Station) or Fish Hold. Actively managing the hauling or setting of lines.
*   **Deckhand:** Main Deck (Hauling Station) or Fish Hold. Executing heavy labor.
*   **Leo (Galley Boy):** Galley (preparing 3-4 meals/day), cleaning communal areas, or running errands across the ship. Often isolated in his bunk when off-shift.
