# Book 1 Timeline: The Galley Boy's Debt

**Duration:** A short, initial offshore run (approximately 4-6 days).
**Start Location:** Barnegat Light, NJ (Boarding).
**End Location:** Belford, NJ (Mid-Route Port Offload).

| Chapter | Location & Narrative Focus | Sea Conditions & Environment |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **1** | **Barnegat Light, NJ.** Docked. Leo boards, signs the contract, and meets the crew. | Calm harbor waters. Smells of diesel and old salt. |
| **2** | Leaving Barnegat Light, heading into the Mid-Atlantic shelf. First Galley Shift. | **10-foot swells.** Rough initial transition. Coastal chop makes working extremely difficult for a greenhorn. |
| **3-4** | Offshore Mid-Atlantic. The Fish Hold Reveal and Contract Enforcement. | Average offshore conditions: **2 to 5 ft seas.** The focus is internal (the fish hold). |
| **5-6** | Open ocean, continuous fishing operations. Days blur. | Variable weather. Swells stabilize around **3 to 4 ft.** The physical grind sets in. |
| **7-8** | Heavy fishing zone. The "Money" Mishap and subsequent claiming. | **2 to 5 ft seas.** High-tension line operations require stable, but active waters. |
| **9** | **Belford, NJ.** Approaching harbor for offload. | Calming coastal waters, busy harbor traffic. |
