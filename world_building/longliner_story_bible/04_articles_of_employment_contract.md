# ARTICLES OF MARITIME EMPLOYMENT: F-CLASS LONGLINER

**VESSEL:** Adapted 60’ Steel Pelagic Longliner (F-Designation Refit)
**PORT OF ORIGIN:** Barnegat Light, New Jersey
**EMPLOYEE:** Leo (Designation: Super-Greenhorn / Galley Boy)
**AGE VERIFICATION:** Confirmed 18+ via State Identification.

### I. SCOPE OF LABOR
The Employee agrees to perform all duties assigned by the Captain, First Mate, or Deck Boss. Primary responsibilities include:
* **Galley Management:** Maintenance of high-capacity dry storage and refrigeration units (+40% capacity).
* **Caloric Prep:** Preparation of massive caloric intake required for Futanari biology.
* **General Maintenance:** Cleaning of heavy-duty hygiene stations and reinforced crew berths.

### II. COMPENSATION & TRANSPORT
In lieu of standard wages, the Vessel provides:
* **Safe Passage:** Transport along the Mid-Atlantic Run to specified ports (Belford, Point Pleasant, or Atlantic City).
* **Subsistence:** Access to ship stores and specialized medical/hygiene kits.

### III. HIERARCHY & CONDUCT
The Vessel operates as a closed ecosystem.
* **Absolute Authority:** The Captain’s word is Law.
* **Rank Seniority:** The Employee is the lowest-ranking entity on the vessel, existing outside the Futanari power dynamic until integrated.
* **Insubordination:** Any failure to perform, or any "debt" incurred through negligence, will be handled internally.

### IV. THE "PUT IT RIGHT" ADDENDUM (CNC CLAUSE)
By signing these Articles, the Employee explicitly consents to the **F-Designation Discipline Protocol**. This protocol dictates that all professional or social friction is to be "put right" through physical settlement.

**1. Debt Accrual:** Debts are issued at the discretion of the Deck Boss, Engineer, or First Mate for errors in labor, attitude, or perceived weakness.

**2. Settlement (Free Use Agreement):**
To ensure ship cohesion and "aggressive downtime" management, the Employee agrees to serve as a physical outlet for the crew. Settlement methods include, but are not limited to:
* **The Wall Pin:** Engineering-specific disciplinary positioning.
* **Deck Claims:** Physical enforcement of labor debts via the Deck Boss or Deckhands.
* **Breeding Legacy:** Ultimate claim by the Captain for lineage purposes (MPG-01).

**3. Safety & Boundary Protocols (KDP Compliance Shield):**
Despite the absolute nature of the power exchange, physical safety is paramount for crew functionality.
* **The Safeword:** Due to the extreme noise levels of the engine room and open deck, standard verbal safewords are invalid. The Employee is issued a mandatory *Visual Safeword* (a specific, unbroken double-tap to the dominant shoulder of the superior). Use of this gesture immediately suspends the "Put it Right" protocol without penalty.
* **Mandatory Restoration (Aftercare):** Following any settlement, the claiming superior is legally bound by ship charter to provide hydration, core temperature regulation, and grounding contact until the Employee is cleared for active duty.

### V. TERMINATION OF CONTRACT
This contract is binding until the Vessel docks and the Captain clears the Employee’s ledger. Attempting to "jump ship" prior to settlement constitutes a breach of maritime law, granting the crew full rights to retrieve and "re-integrate" the Employee via any means necessary.
